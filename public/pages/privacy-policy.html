Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

SNIPMETA ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • URLs: We collect Instagram and Threads URLs that you paste into our application for the purpose of downloading videos.
  • Optional Contact Information: If you contact us, we may collect your email address and messages.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Server Logs: IP address, browser type, operating system, and timestamps
  • Cookies: We use cookies for theme preferences and session management
  • Analytics: We may use Google Analytics to track user behavior

2.3 Third-Party Services

  • We may use third-party services to process video downloads and analyze performance
  • These services have their own privacy policies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and download videos as requested
  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Prevent abuse and secure our platform
  • Send technical notices and support messages
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests

4. Data Retention

We do not store downloaded videos on our servers. Server logs are retained for up to 30 days for security and analytics purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Store your theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Maintain your session
  • Analyze website usage

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please review their privacy policies before providing any information.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to opt-out of certain data uses

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@yourdomain.com

10. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the EU, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Your personal data is processed lawfully based on your consent or legitimate interests.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: support@yourdomain.com
Website: https://yourdomain.com

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
