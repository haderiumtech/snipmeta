How to Download Instagram & Threads Videos

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Find the Video

Go to Instagram or Threads and find the video you want to download. This can be a:

  • Instagram Post
  • Instagram Reel
  • Instagram TV (IGTV)
  • Threads Video

Step 2: Copy the Link

On Mobile:

  1. Open the video on Instagram or Threads
  2. Tap the three dots (⋮) menu
  3. Select "Copy Link"

On Desktop:

  1. Right-click on the video
  2. Select "Copy Link" or copy the URL from the address bar

Step 3: Paste on InstaTube

  1. Paste the URL into the input field on our website
  2. Click the "Download" button
  3. Wait for the video to be processed (usually under 10 seconds)

Step 4: Download

  1. Once processed, you'll see a preview with a download button
  2. Click "Download MP4" to save the video to your device
  3. The video will be saved to your Downloads folder

Troubleshooting

❌ "Invalid URL" Error

  • Make sure you copied the entire link including "https://"
  • Verify the video is public (private videos cannot be downloaded)
  • Try clearing your browser cache and refreshing

❌ Download Fails

  • Check your internet connection
  • Make sure the video is not deleted
  • Try a different video to confirm the issue
  • Contact us if the problem persists

❌ Video Won't Play After Download

  • Try using a different media player (VLC Media Player recommended)
  • Update your browser and operating system
  • Check that your device has enough storage space

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this app legal?

The app itself is legal. However, downloading copyrighted content without permission may violate terms of service. We recommend only downloading videos you have the right to download.

Can I download stories?

Stories expire after 24 hours and are more difficult to download. Our tool is optimized for permanent posts, reels, and threads videos.

What video formats are supported?

We support MP4 format for all Instagram posts, reels, IGTV, and Threads videos.

Is there a video file size limit?

No size limit, but very large videos may take longer to process.

Do you store my downloads?

No. Videos are downloaded directly from Instagram/Threads to your device. We don't store any files.
